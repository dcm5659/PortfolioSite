Project Description

Snowballin' is a casual game built in Unity. It plays similar to Katamari Damacy as the player rolls around picking up items with the ball. The game has two modes: time attack, where the player fights against melting by picking up items, and adventure mode, where they can explore the map trying to get the best score.
